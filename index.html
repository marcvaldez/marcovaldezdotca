About Me

Two years ago, I moved to Fredericton, New Brunswick, Canada to start a new phase in my career after being a successful software developer in the Philippines. Being a self-taught programmer, I enrolled in the Information Technology: Programmer-Analyst program at New Brunswick Community College (NBCC) to bolster my credentials and improve the existing technical and interpersonal skills I have gained through the years.

I have worked on a wide variety of projects involving Web and Mobile Application Developement, Database Design, and Systems Architecture. As a Senior Full-Stack Software Developer, I collaborated with several agile teams based in the United States, Canada, and the Philippines. Some of the things I experienced in my career include creating an enterprise mobile app to assist salespeople, training teams of developers in adopting Behavior-Driven Development, and architecting a distributed ETL process for processing high-volume message queues.

I am open to opportunities in Fredericton or remote work around the globe. I am available to work after my graduation on June 2021.

I can be reached through Facebook, LinkedIn, or my email at marc_fv@yahoo.com.
